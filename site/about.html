About Us

We’re a team rebels and artists. Engineers and geeks. Small and medium business owners. We build things. Some of us are young. Others have a few wrinkles. We’re a loving and daring bunch. A dream chasing crew. Always looking for the next challenge.

We’ve been involved in dozens of businesses bringing in experience from various industries. Each time we picked up a few lessons from the hardships of running our own company. A lot of them are up to us: communication, transparency and a lot of important things we blog about. Others were out of our control. Like business banking.

It’s expensive. It’s slow. The paperwork. The branches. The lack of innovation. The lack of overview of the business. Where’s the 21st century? Where’s the modern online banking? Data driven banking. Simple banking. Fast and cost-efficient banking.

Being the daring bunch we are, we decided to change that. Our Mission:

We’re on a mission to enable businesses to do more. So that you can create more value for your clients, for your employees, for your business while saving time and money, making better, faster and data-driven decisions. That’s why we’re building the greatest bank for SMEs. We’re building Penta: a cost-efficient, fast and simple bank enabling your business to do more, spend less and make smarter data driven decisions.
